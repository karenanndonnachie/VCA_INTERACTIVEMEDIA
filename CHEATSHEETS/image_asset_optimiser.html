How to use this tool

  • Drop or select an image to begin.
  • Crop by dragging on the image, then click Crop.
  • Resize, rename, and download your image.
  • For Cursor images, you can remove the background and add an outline.
  • Use Reset to undo cropping or background removal.
  • The page background will tile with your uploaded image for fun!
